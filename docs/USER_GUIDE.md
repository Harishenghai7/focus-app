## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit the Focus app and click **Sign Up**
2. Enter your email address and create a strong password
   - Password must be at least 8 characters
   - Include uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character
3. Verify your email by clicking the link sent to your inbox
4. Complete your profile setup:
   - Choose a unique username (3-30 characters)
   - Add your full name
   - Upload a profile picture (optional)
   - Write a bio (optional, max 150 characters)

### Logging In

1. Enter your email and password
2. Click **Log In**
3. If you enabled two-factor authentication, enter your 6-digit code

**Forgot Password?**
- Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
- Enter your email address
- Check your email for a reset link
- Create a new password

## Account Management

### Editing Your Profile

1. Go to your profile by clicking your avatar
2. Click **Edit Profile**
3. Update any of the following:
   - Profile picture
   - Username
   - Full name
   - Bio
   - Website
4. Click **Save** to apply changes

### Privacy Settings

**Making Your Account Private:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Privacy**
2. Toggle **Private Account** on
3. New followers will need your approval

**Managing Follow Requests:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Follow Requests**
2. View pending requests
3. Click **Approve** or **Reject** for each request

### Account Security

**Enabling Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Security**
2. Click **Enable 2FA**
3. Scan the QR code with an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy)
4. Enter the 6-digit code to confirm
5. Save your backup codes in a safe place

**Managing Active Sessions:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Security** → **Active Sessions**
2. View all devices where you're logged in
3. Click **Log Out** on any session to end it
4. Click **Log Out All Devices** to end all sessions except current

## Creating Content

### Creating a Post

1. Click the **+** button in the navigation bar
2. Select **Post**
3. Choose up to 10 photos or videos
4. Add a caption (max 500 characters)
   - Use **#hashtags** to categorize your post
   - Use **@mentions** to tag other users
5. Add a location (optional)
6. Click **Share** to publish

**Creating a Carousel:**
- Select multiple images/videos (2-10 items)
- Drag to reorder them
- Each item can be an image or video

**Saving Drafts:**
- Your post is auto-saved every 30 seconds
- Access drafts from the Create page
- Click on a draft to continue editing

**Scheduling Posts:**
1. Create your post as normal
2. Click **Schedule** instead of Share
3. Select date and time
4. Your post will be published automatically

### Creating Boltz (Short Videos)

1. Click the **+** button
2. Select **Boltz**
3. Record or upload a vertical video (max 60 seconds)
4. Add a caption
5. Click **Share**

**Boltz Tips:**
- Hold the record button to capture video
- Use good lighting for better quality
- Keep videos engaging and concise
- Add trending hashtags for more visibility

### Creating Flash Stories

1. Click your profile picture with the **+** icon
2. Take a photo/video or select from gallery
3. Add text, stickers, or drawings (optional)
4. Choose audience:
   - **All Followers** - Everyone who follows you
   - **Close Friends** - Only your close friends list
5. Click **Share to Story**

**Flash Features:**
- Stories expire after 24 hours
- See who viewed your story
- Reply to stories via direct message
- Save stories to highlights for permanent display

**Creating Highlights:**
1. Go to your profile
2. Click **New Highlight**
3. Select stories to include
4. Choose a cover image
5. Name your highlight
6. Click **Create**

## Interacting with Content

### Liking Content

- **Double-tap** any post or Boltz to like it
- Or click the **heart icon** below the content
- Click again to unlike

### Commenting

1. Click the **comment icon** below a post
2. Type your comment (max 500 characters)
3. Use **@username** to mention someone
4. Click **Post** to publish

**Replying to Comments:**
- Click **Reply** under any comment
- Your reply will be nested under the original comment

**Comment Reactions:**
- Long-press any comment
- Select an emoji reaction
- See all reactions by clicking the reaction count

### Saving Posts

1. Click the **bookmark icon** below a post
2. Access saved posts from your profile → **Saved**

**Creating Collections:**
1. Go to **Saved** → **Collections**
2. Click **New Collection**
3. Name your collection
4. Add posts to organize your saved content

### Sharing Content

1. Click the **share icon** below any post
2. Choose:
   - **Send via DM** - Share with specific users
   - **Share to Story** - Repost to your Flash story
   - **Copy Link** - Copy the post URL

## Messaging

### Sending Direct Messages

1. Click the **message icon** in the navigation
2. Click **New Message**
3. Select a recipient
4. Type your message and press **Send**

**Message Features:**
- Send photos and videos
- Send voice messages (hold the mic icon)
- Reply to specific messages
- React to messages with emojis
- Delete messages (for you or everyone)

**Voice Messages:**
1. Hold the **microphone icon**
2. Speak your message
3. Release to send, or slide to cancel

### Group Chats

**Creating a Group:**
1. Click **New Message** → **New Group**
2. Select up to 50 members
3. Name your group
4. Add a group photo (optional)
5. Click **Create**

**Managing Groups:**
- **Add members**: Click group name → Add Members
- **Remove members**: Click group name → Members → Remove (admin only)
- **Leave group**: Click group name → Leave Group
- **Change group name/photo**: Click group name → Edit (admin only)

### Message Settings

**Muting Conversations:**
1. Open the conversation
2. Click the **info icon**
3. Toggle **Mute Notifications**

**Deleting Conversations:**
1. Long-press the conversation
2. Click **Delete**
3. Confirm deletion

## Audio & Video Calls

### Making a Call

1. Open a direct message conversation
2. Click the **phone icon** for audio call
3. Or click the **video icon** for video call
4. Wait for the recipient to answer

### During a Call

**Controls:**
- **Mute/Unmute**: Toggle your microphone
- **Camera On/Off**: Toggle your camera (video calls)
- **Switch Camera**: Switch between front/back camera
- **End Call**: Hang up the call

**Picture-in-Picture:**
- Minimize the call to continue using the app
- The call window stays on top
- Click to return to full screen

### Call History

1. Go to **Messages** → **Calls**
2. View all incoming, outgoing, and missed calls
3. Click any call to call back

## Privacy & Security

### Blocking Users

1. Go to the user's profile
2. Click the **three dots** menu
3. Select **Block**
4. Confirm the action

**What happens when you block someone:**
- They can't see your posts or stories
- They can't message you
- They can't find your profile in search
- Existing follows are removed

**Unblocking:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Privacy** → **Blocked Accounts**
2. Find the user
3. Click **Unblock**

### Activity Status

**Hiding Your Activity:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Privacy**
2. Toggle **Show Activity Status** off
3. Others won't see when you're online

**Note:** If you turn off activity status, you won't see others' status either.

### Close Friends

**Managing Close Friends List:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Close Friends**
2. Click **Add** next to users to include them
3. Click **Remove** to take them off the list
4. Share Flash stories exclusively with this group

## Search & Discovery

### Searching

1. Click the **search icon** in the navigation
2. Type your query:
   - **Users**: Search by username or name
   - **Hashtags**: Search by #tag
   - **Content**: Search by caption text

**Search Tips:**
- Recent searches appear first
- Click **X** to clear search history
- Tap any result to view it

### Explore Page

1. Click the **explore icon** (compass)
2. Browse trending content
3. Filter by category:
   - **For You**: Personalized recommendations
   - **Photos**: Image posts
   - **Videos**: Video posts
   - **Boltz**: Short-form videos

### Following Hashtags

1. Search for a hashtag
2. Click the hashtag name
3. Click **Follow**
4. See posts with this hashtag in your feed

## Settings

### Notification Preferences

1. Go to **Settings** → **Notifications**
2. Toggle notifications for:
   - Likes
   - Comments
   - New followers
   - Messages
   - Mentions
   - Live videos

**Push Notifications:**
- Enable in your browser/device settings
- Focus will ask for permission on first use

### Theme Settings

**Switching Between Light and Dark Mode:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Appearance**
2. Select:
   - **Light**: Bright theme
   - **Dark**: Dark theme
   - **System**: Match your device settings

### Data Management

**Downloading Your Data:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Privacy & Security**
2. Click **Download Data**
3. Confirm your password
4. Receive a download link via email within 48 hours

**Deleting Your Account:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Account**
2. Click **Delete Account**
3. Read the information carefully
4. Enter your password
5. Confirm deletion

**Note:** Account deletion has a 30-day grace period. You can cancel within 30 days by logging in.

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Can't Log In:**
- Check your email and password are correct
- Ensure your email is verified
- Try resetting your password
- Clear browser cache and cookies

**Posts Not Loading:**
- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser

**Upload Failing:**
- Check file size (max 100MB for posts)
- Ensure file format is supported (JPG, PNG, MP4, MOV)
- Check your internet connection
- Try compressing the file

**Notifications Not Working:**
- Check notification permissions in browser/device settings
- Ensure notifications are enabled in Focus settings
- Try logging out and back in

**Video Not Playing:**
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Ensure your browser supports HTML5 video
- Update your browser to the latest version

### Getting Help

**Report a Problem:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Help**
2. Click **Report a Problem**
3. Describe the issue
4. Include screenshots if possible
5. Submit the report

**Contact Support:**
- Email: support@focus.app
- Response time: 24-48 hours

## Tips for Best Experience

### Content Creation Tips

1. **Use Good Lighting**: Natural light works best for photos and videos
2. **Write Engaging Captions**: Ask questions, tell stories, use emojis
3. **Use Hashtags Wisely**: 5-10 relevant hashtags per post
4. **Post Consistently**: Regular posting keeps your audience engaged
5. **Engage with Others**: Like and comment on others' content

### Privacy Best Practices

1. **Review Privacy Settings**: Regularly check who can see your content
2. **Be Selective with Followers**: Approve follow requests carefully
3. **Don't Share Sensitive Info**: Avoid posting personal information
4. **Use Strong Passwords**: Change your password regularly
5. **Enable 2FA**: Add an extra layer of security

### Growing Your Audience

1. **Post Quality Content**: Focus on high-quality, engaging posts
2. **Use Trending Hashtags**: Participate in trending conversations
3. **Engage with Community**: Comment and interact with others
4. **Post at Peak Times**: Share when your audience is most active
5. **Be Authentic**: Share genuine content that reflects your personality
