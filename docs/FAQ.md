# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is Focus?

Focus is a modern social media platform that combines the best features of photo sharing, short-form videos, and ephemeral stories. Connect with friends, share moments, and discover content that matters to you.

### Is Focus free to use?

Yes, Focus is completely free to use. There are no subscription fees or hidden costs.

### What devices can I use Focus on?

Focus works on:
- Web browsers (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge)
- iOS devices (iPhone, iPad)
- Android devices (phones and tablets)
- Desktop computers (Windows, Mac, Linux)

### How is Focus different from other social media platforms?

Focus combines multiple content formats in one app:
- **Posts**: Traditional photo/video sharing with carousels
- **Boltz**: Short-form vertical videos (like TikTok/Reels)
- **Flash**: 24-hour ephemeral stories with highlights
- **Messaging**: Direct and group chats with voice messages
- **Calls**: Built-in audio and video calling

## Account & Login

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a reset link
4. Click the link and create a new password
5. Log in with your new password

### Why do I need to verify my email?

Email verification ensures:
- Account security
- Password recovery capability
- Protection against spam accounts
- Compliance with platform policies

### Can I change my username?

Yes, you can change your username at any time:
1. Go to your profile
2. Click "Edit Profile"
3. Change your username
4. Click "Save"

**Note**: Your old username becomes available for others to use.

### How do I delete my account?

1. Go to Settings → Account
2. Click "Delete Account"
3. Read the information carefully
4. Enter your password
5. Confirm deletion

**Important**: You have 30 days to cancel the deletion by logging in. After 30 days, all your data is permanently deleted.

### Can I have multiple accounts?

While technically possible, we recommend using one account per person for the best experience. Multiple accounts may be subject to additional verification.

## Privacy & Security

### How do I make my account private?

1. Go to Settings → Privacy
2. Toggle "Private Account" on
3. New followers will need your approval

When your account is private:
- Only approved followers see your posts
- Your posts don't appear in Explore
- People must request to follow you

### What happens when I block someone?

When you block a user:
- They can't see your profile, posts, or stories
- They can't message you
- They can't find you in search
- Existing follows are removed
- They won't be notified of the block

### Can blocked users see my old comments on their posts?

No, your comments on their posts will be hidden from them, but may still be visible to others.

### How do I report inappropriate content?

1. Click the three dots menu on the post/comment
2. Select "Report"
3. Choose a reason
4. Provide additional details (optional)
5. Submit the report

Our team reviews all reports within 24-48 hours.

### Is my data secure?

Yes, Focus takes security seriously:
- All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS)
- Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
- Two-factor authentication is available
- Regular security audits are performed
- Row-level security protects your data

### Can Focus employees see my messages?

No, Focus employees cannot read your private messages. Messages are encrypted and only accessible to you and the recipients.

## Content Creation

### What file formats are supported?

**Images**: JPG, JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP
**Videos**: MP4, MOV, AVI, WebM

### What are the file size limits?

- **Posts**: Up to 100MB total (all images/videos combined)
- **Boltz**: Up to 50MB per video
- **Flash Stories**: Up to 50MB per story
- **Profile Picture**: Up to 5MB

### How many photos can I include in a carousel post?

You can include 2-10 images or videos in a single carousel post.

### Can I edit a post after publishing?

Yes, you can edit:
- Caption
- Location
- Alt text

You cannot:
- Add or remove media
- Change media order

To edit: Click the three dots menu on your post → Edit

### How long do Flash stories last?

Flash stories automatically expire after 24 hours. You can:
- Save them to highlights before they expire
- View expired stories in your archive
- Delete them manually at any time

### Can I schedule posts?

Yes! When creating a post:
1. Click "Schedule" instead of "Share"
2. Select date and time
3. Your post will be published automatically

View scheduled posts in your profile under "Scheduled".

### Why is my video quality reduced?

Videos are automatically compressed to:
- Reduce upload time
- Save storage space
- Improve playback performance
- Reduce data usage for viewers

For best quality:
- Upload in 1080p or higher
- Use good lighting
- Keep file size under 50MB

## Interactions

### Why can't I like or comment on a post?

Possible reasons:
- The account is private and you don't follow them
- The user has blocked you
- Comments are disabled on that post
- You've been rate-limited (too many actions too quickly)

### How do I turn off comments on my post?

1. Go to your post
2. Click the three dots menu
3. Select "Turn Off Comments"

You can turn them back on at any time.

### Can I see who saved my post?

No, saves are private. You can only see the total save count, not individual users who saved it.

### What's the difference between saving and liking?

- **Like**: Shows appreciation, visible to others, notifies the creator
- **Save**: Bookmarks for later, private, doesn't notify the creator

### How do I pin a comment on my post?

1. Find the comment you want to pin
2. Click the three dots menu
3. Select "Pin Comment"

Only post owners can pin comments. You can pin one comment per post.

### Can I unlike or unsave content?

Yes, click the heart or bookmark icon again to remove the like or save.

## Messaging

### How do I know if someone read my message?

If read receipts are enabled, you'll see:
- **Sent**: One checkmark
- **Delivered**: Two checkmarks
- **Read**: Two blue checkmarks

### Can I delete a message after sending?

Yes, you can delete messages:
- **Delete for Me**: Removes from your view only
- **Delete for Everyone**: Removes for all participants (within 1 hour)

### How many people can be in a group chat?

Group chats support up to 50 members.

### Can I leave a group chat?

Yes, click the group name → Leave Group. You can be re-added by any group member.

### What are voice messages?

Voice messages let you send audio recordings instead of text:
1. Hold the microphone icon
2. Speak your message
3. Release to send

### Why can't I message someone?

Possible reasons:
- They have blocked you
- Their account is private and you don't follow them
- They have restricted messages to followers only
- Their account has been deactivated

## Calls

### Are calls free?

Yes, all audio and video calls are free. Standard data charges from your carrier may apply.

### Why is my call quality poor?

Call quality depends on:
- Internet connection speed
- Network stability
- Device performance
- Distance between participants

**Tips for better quality**:
- Use WiFi instead of cellular data
- Close other apps
- Ensure good lighting for video calls
- Move closer to your router

### Can I make group calls?

Currently, calls are one-on-one only. Group calling is planned for a future update.

### How do I switch between front and back camera?

During a video call, tap the camera switch icon to toggle between cameras.

### Are calls recorded?

No, Focus does not record calls. Calls are peer-to-peer and not stored on our servers.

## Search & Discovery

### How does the Explore page work?

The Explore page shows:
- Trending content based on engagement
- Personalized recommendations based on your interests
- Popular posts from accounts you don't follow

### Why don't I see certain posts in my feed?

Your feed shows:
- Posts from accounts you follow
- Posts in reverse chronological order
- Only posts from public accounts or approved follows

### How do I follow a hashtag?

1. Search for the hashtag
2. Click on the hashtag name
3. Click "Follow"
4. Posts with this hashtag appear in your feed

### Can I mute hashtags?

Not currently, but you can:
- Unfollow hashtags you're following
- Use the "Not Interested" option on posts

### How do trending hashtags work?

Hashtags trend based on:
- Recent usage frequency
- Engagement on posts using the hashtag
- Growth rate of usage

## Notifications

### Why am I not receiving notifications?

Check:
1. Notification permissions in your browser/device settings
2. Focus notification settings (Settings → Notifications)
3. Do Not Disturb mode on your device
4. Browser notification settings

### Can I customize which notifications I receive?

Yes, go to Settings → Notifications and toggle:
- Likes
- Comments
- New followers
- Messages
- Mentions
- Follow requests

### What are push notifications?

Push notifications appear even when Focus is closed:
- New messages
- Missed calls
- Important interactions

Enable in Settings → Notifications → Push Notifications

### How do I mute notifications from a specific person?

1. Go to their profile
2. Click the three dots menu
3. Select "Mute Notifications"

You'll still see their posts, but won't receive notifications.

## Technical Issues

### The app is running slowly. What should I do?

Try:
1. Refresh the page
2. Clear browser cache and cookies
3. Close other tabs/apps
4. Restart your browser
5. Check your internet connection
6. Update your browser

### Images aren't loading. How do I fix this?

1. Check your internet connection
2. Refresh the page
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try a different browser
5. Disable browser extensions temporarily

### I'm getting an error message. What does it mean?

Common errors:
- **"Network Error"**: Check your internet connection
- **"Session Expired"**: Log in again
- **"Rate Limit Exceeded"**: Wait a few minutes before trying again
- **"File Too Large"**: Compress your file or choose a smaller one
- **"Invalid Format"**: Use a supported file format

### The video player isn't working. What should I do?

1. Ensure your browser supports HTML5 video
2. Update your browser to the latest version
3. Disable hardware acceleration in browser settings
4. Try a different browser
5. Check if the video file is corrupted

### How do I clear my cache?

**Chrome**: Settings → Privacy → Clear Browsing Data
**Safari**: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
**Firefox**: Options → Privacy → Clear Data

## Features & Functionality

### What are Close Friends?

Close Friends is a special list of people who can see your private Flash stories. Create stories visible only to this group.

### What are Story Highlights?

Highlights are collections of Flash stories saved permanently to your profile. They don't expire after 24 hours.

### Can I download my posts?

Yes, click the three dots menu on your post → Download. You can also download all your data from Settings → Privacy & Security → Download Data.

### What is the difference between Posts and Boltz?

- **Posts**: Traditional photo/video sharing, up to 10 items, any aspect ratio
- **Boltz**: Short vertical videos (max 60 seconds), full-screen viewing, swipe navigation

### Can I share someone else's post?

Yes, click the share icon and choose:
- Send via DM
- Share to your Story
- Copy link

### What are Collections?

Collections help organize your saved posts into categories. Create collections like "Recipes", "Travel", "Inspiration", etc.

## Account Management

### How do I change my email address?

Currently, email addresses cannot be changed. You would need to create a new account with the new email.

### Can I merge two accounts?

No, account merging is not currently supported. You can manually transfer content by downloading and re-uploading.

### How do I verify my account?

Account verification (blue checkmark) is currently limited to:
- Public figures
- Brands
- Organizations

Verification requests will be available in a future update.

### What happens to my content if I deactivate my account?

When you delete your account:
- Your profile becomes invisible immediately
- You have 30 days to cancel by logging in
- After 30 days, all content is permanently deleted
- Usernames become available for others

## Best Practices

### How often should I post?

There's no perfect frequency, but consistency matters:
- **Daily**: Keeps you top-of-mind
- **3-4 times/week**: Good balance
- **Weekly**: Minimum for staying relevant

Quality over quantity always wins.

### When is the best time to post?

Best times vary by audience, but generally:
- **Weekdays**: 11am-1pm, 7pm-9pm
- **Weekends**: 9am-11am, 5pm-7pm

Check your analytics to find when your audience is most active.

### How do I grow my followers?

1. Post high-quality, engaging content
2. Use relevant hashtags (5-10 per post)
3. Engage with others' content
4. Post consistently
5. Respond to comments
6. Collaborate with others
7. Share authentic, valuable content

### Should I use hashtags?

Yes! Hashtags help people discover your content. Best practices:
- Use 5-10 relevant hashtags
- Mix popular and niche hashtags
- Create a branded hashtag
- Research trending hashtags
- Don't use banned or spam hashtags
